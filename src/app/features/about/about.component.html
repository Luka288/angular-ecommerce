frequently asked questions

One of the most frequently asked questions we receive is about delivery time. Typically, items are delivered within an estimated timeframe of 1 to 2 weeks.

Our products are sourced directly from official manufacturers, ensuring authenticity and quality. We proudly serve as official resellers for the brands featured in our collection, offering you genuine items from trusted sources.

Return Policy: We accept returns within 30 days if the item has a manufacturing defect. In such cases, we will issue a full refund.
Warranty: The warranty for our products is provided by the official manufacturer, and the coverage period varies depending on the item.
